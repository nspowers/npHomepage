Allen's Ridge Ranch Videos

North Greenville University

Our advertising course team created videos to promote the products offered by Allen's Ridge Ranch

 
Services: Planning, Postproduction Editing, Graphics integration
Deliverables: Four promotional videos

Origin

Allen's Ridge Ranch, located in the northern portion of South Carolina asked North Greenville University if marketing students could partner with the ranch to increase promotions of its offerings. Our team scripted, shot, and delivered four videos ranging from one to two minutes each, for the ranch's four core products, alpaca fiber, meat, great danes, and Friesian sport horses.
