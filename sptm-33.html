Sport Management Communications

North Greenville University

The College of Business requested media to promote its Sport Management program to prospective students.

 
Services: Graphic Design, Composition, Photography, Prepres, Video Editing and Graphics
Deliverables: Brochures, Program Presentation Portfolio, Program Track Posters, Invitational Video

Origin

The Sport Management program needed to showcase its unique blend of sports studies within a business context. University partners wanted professional tools for communicating with current and future students and recruiters.

Developing Program Objectives

I interviewed the program director and current students to learn the strenghts of the program and what opportunities the program's three tracks could supply to prospective students. Working closely with the program's director, I developed print, web, and video assets to support the program's student ambassadors, admissions recruiters, and faculty communicate the program's career opportunities.

I designed a portfolio for presenting program information to campus visiors. With a cover depicting imagery from the program's three tracks as well as inserts and a slot for the program director's business card, the portfolio packages program documents. The reverse side shows the University's seal and program contact information.

Project Management

I coordinated the collection of imagery for the project, including scheduling photo shoots of students, photographing events, and catagorizing submissions from admissions and athetic photographers. I also filmed and edited a promotional video narriated by the program's director.

Medium: Print

Tools:  Adobe After Effects, InDesign, Illustrator, Premier Pro, and Photoshop; HTML and CSS
