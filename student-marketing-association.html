Student Marketing Association

North Greenville University

In my leadership roles in my university's Student Marketing Association, I managed the organizaiton's communications and brand.

 
Services: Graphic Design, Photography, Scheduling Meetings, Speaker Recruitment
Deliverables: Brochures, Posters, Website

Origin

I designed a logo for the Student Marketing Association. The logo unites the university and organization's initials. The red, oblique type of the logo provides visual emphasis to the dynamic and forward-leaning nature of the student organization.

Developing Assets

SMA's goal is to expose students to the diversity that exists in the marketing field. I created a banner for SMA's website and Facebook page with the tagline, “Get a front row seat to explore the world of marketing”.

I also designed flyers for the meetings, a brochure for prospective students, and SMA's website.

Medium: Web

Tools:  Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Sketch, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP
