Branding Internship

Allen's Ridge Ranch

Allen's Ridge is a ranch offering premium quality alpaca, Dexter Cattle, Freisian Sport Horses, and Great Danes. The ranch needed a brand to market its offerings.

 
Services: Copywriting, Graphic Design, Web Design
Deliverables: Brandmark, Brochures, Business Cards, Product Labeling, Social Media, Trade Show Banner

Origin

Stately black Friesian sport horses, like those from King Arthur films? They're out in the pasture. Prized Huacaya alpacas, whose fiber is so luxuriant that you wish everything you wore was woven with alpaca? The Ranch has those, too. Proud Great Danes trot up to greet you and Irish Dexter Cattle roam the fields.

Design

Typography

The typography conveys the rustic, Western ambiance of the ranch. Chunckfive paired with Roboslab as brand and body copy, supports the ranch's positioning.

Color

Earth and animal tones provide the foundation for the palette. Rich browns and reds illuminate the quality of the ranch's products. A touch of golden yellow, selected from the sheen of alpaca firm provides the occasional highlight.

Brandmark

The brandmark combines the ranch's key offerings, Friesian Sport horses, alpaca, Great Danes, and Dexter Cattle. The circular composition uses Gestalt to reveal the different animals.

Unifying the ranch's offerings under one brand increases awareness. The distinct brandmark advertises the ranch's other offerings.

Medium: Web

Tools:  Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Lightroom
